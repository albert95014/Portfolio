Genuary 2023

Daily exercises in learning TouchDesigner

Background

Genuary happens every year during January where creative coders, technologists, and artists alike follow a prompt and post a piece of generative art each day of the month. I used the prompts as inspiration for me to learn and practice TouchDesigner, as its premise of mapping data with visuals was something I wanted to dive deeper into. Every day I tried to go through a new tutorial on YouTube (thanks Bileam Tschepe and his amazing guides!) to experiment with the various tools in TouchDesigner's interface.

Below are some selected pieces that came out of my Genuary 2023. The full month's worth of art can be found on Instagram.
